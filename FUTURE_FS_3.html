💼 Client Proposal Pitch

🏢 Business Overview

This CRM system is designed for a small digital marketing agency that receives client inquiries through website contact forms, social media ads, and referrals. The agency provides website development, branding, and marketing services.

❗ Problem Statement

The business previously handled inquiries through email and WhatsApp, making it difficult to track which leads were contacted, which required follow-ups, and which converted into clients. This resulted in missed opportunities and disorganized client tracking.

✅ Solution Provided

The Mini CRM system centralizes all incoming leads into one dashboard. It allows status updates (New → Contacted → Converted), follow-up tracking, and analytics monitoring.

📈 Business Impact

This solution improves lead response time, increases conversion rates, prevents loss of potential clients, and gives the business owner full visibility over their sales pipeline.
